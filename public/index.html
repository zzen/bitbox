Setup steps

  1. Add the following to your domain:
    yourdomain.com CNAME bitbox.com bitbox-verify.yourdomain.com CNAME bitbox.com
  2. Download this file and place it into your DropBox Public folder.
  3. Right-click on the file from previous step and select Dropbox > "Copy Public Link"
  4. Come back here, fill out your domain, paste the dropbox file link and press the "Activate" button.

Note: After these setup steps are successfully completed, it is recommended that you remove the bitbox-verify.yourdomain.com record from your domain and the file-sample.txt from your DropBox public folder. The yourdomain.com entry must stay however, otherwise the alias will not be functional.
